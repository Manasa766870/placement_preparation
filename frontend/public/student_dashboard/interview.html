Interview Preparation

🧑‍💼 HR Interview

Tell me about yourself
Start with your education → skills → projects → end with career goal.
Why should we hire you?
Combine skills + projects + attitude: “I have strong DSA basics and built projects like X, and I’m eager to learn.”
What are your strengths?
Example: Problem-solving, consistency, teamwork (with example).
What is your weakness?
Mention real weakness + improvement action.
Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
Talk about growth, skills, and contributing to company.

💻 Technical Interview

What is normalization?
Removes redundancy in DB.
What is indexing?
Improves search speed in DB.
What is deadlock?
Processes waiting forever.
Process vs Thread?
Process = independent, Thread = lightweight.
What is TCP?
Reliable communication protocol.
What is OOP?
Object-based programming (encapsulation, inheritance).

👨‍💻 Coding Interview

What is time complexity?
Measures performance of algorithm.
What is Big-O?
Worst-case complexity.
Stack vs Queue?
Stack = LIFO, Queue = FIFO.
What is recursion?
Function calling itself.
BFS vs DFS?
BFS = level, DFS = depth.

🎯 Interview Tips

  • Be confident
  • Explain projects clearly
  • Revise DSA + CS
  • Practice daily
